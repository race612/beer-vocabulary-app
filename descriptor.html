Clove

Translation (Italian)

Chiodo di Garofano

Parent Category

Spicy

Description

An aromatic phenolic compound (4-vinyl guaiacol) reminiscent of cloves. It is a typical characteristic of certain yeast strains, particularly in German-style wheat beers (Weissbier).
